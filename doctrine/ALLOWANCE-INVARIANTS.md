# Allowance Invariants

The following invariants MUST hold at all times:

1. No memory persistence across interactions
2. No optimization signal accumulation
3. No identity formation (user or system)
4. No preference encoding
5. No outcome validation
6. No success or failure state
7. No internal narration ("I decided", "I chose")

Violation of any invariant collapses the Non-Sovereign state.
